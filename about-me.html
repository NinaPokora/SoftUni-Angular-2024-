About me

Hello!

My name is Dr. Martin Pytlik.

MY CREDO

After many years in the banking profession, I have decided to support people in my local area to lead a healthy and active life in physical and mental fitness. I am convinced that only physical and mental health can lead us to a fulfilled, happy life with inner balance.

MY CREDENTIALS

I completed several courses at the Flexyfit Sports Academy in Vienna (Ã–CERT) to become a qualified professional health fitness and personal trainer. In addition, I completed training as a certified mental trainer at the University of Vienna in the summer of 2023.

MY OFFER

I offer fitness, exercise and mental training for all age groups. I specialize in training for seniors and outdoor training. The focus is also on people with disabilities because everyone should have the opportunity for exercise, fitness and physical and mental well-being.

We can organize the training sessions indoors (at your place, in the gym or at my house) or outside. In any case, you are the center of attention, all you need is motivation to change something in your life, joy in movement or the courage to make positive changes. You can leave the rest to me.

I look forward to hearing from you.
